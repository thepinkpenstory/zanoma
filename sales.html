FAQ

Common Questions

Fast answers to the things people always ask.

How fast can I be live?
Most clients go live within 24–48 hours of signing up. We handle the setup call, configure your qualification script, and test before launch. No engineering team required.
Does the AI sound like a robot?
No. Our AI uses natural conversational flow trained on thousands of real sales calls. Most prospects don't realize they're talking to an AI until the meeting is already booked.
What if a lead needs a human?
Zanoma detects escalation triggers and hands off to your team via SMS, Slack, or call transfer in real time. You set the rules — we follow them.
Which CRMs do you integrate with?
Native integrations with HubSpot, Salesforce, Go High Level, and Pipedrive. Plus 100+ tools via Zapier and Make for anything else in your stack.
Is there a contract?
No long-term contracts on Growth and Starter plans. Monthly billing, cancel anytime. Enterprise plans include optional annual agreements with volume discounts.
